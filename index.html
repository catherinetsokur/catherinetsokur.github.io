Who I Am

I'm Catherine Tsokur.

I believe that empowered people in effective organisations create impact.

My role as a leader is to create environments where individuals and organisations perform at their best. I help individual contributors, managers, and leaders navigate growth and leadership challenges, and address organisational, operational, and alignment issues that slow down progress.

Main areas of my focus:

  • Growing people and helping them reach their potential
  • Developing leadership capability and high-impact communication
  • Building effective, high-performing development teams
  • Designing organisations and operating models that support execution at scale
  • Creating clarity and strategic alignment around meaningful outcomes
